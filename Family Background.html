Family

The Cannavo name has been a major name in the streets of Boston for years. John Cannavo the original founder of "John P. Cannavo Plumbing and Heating" ran the plumbing game in the 60s-80s. They owned a warehouse in South Boston on Boylston st, owning 12 trucks and having over 50 employees at the time. The legacy of the Cannavo name continues till this day with two trucks and 6 employees, including 3 family members today.
